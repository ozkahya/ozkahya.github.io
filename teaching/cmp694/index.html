Learning objectives

This course aims to cover state-of-the-art applications of graph theoretical methods in computer science, in particular on graph mining.

Resources

  • Introduction to Algorithms by Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, and Clifford Stein.
  • Algorithm Design by Jon Kleinberg, and Eva Tardos.
  • Jeff Erickson's Algorithm Notes (available online)
  • Course Notes for "CS15-251: Great Theoretical Ideas in Computer Science" at CMU (available online)
